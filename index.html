Cloud Sync:

Sign in with your email and password to sync your records across all your devices. Each shooter has their own account and their own private records.

The first time, choose Create Account. After that, use Sign In on any device. Forgot password? emails you a reset link.

Saving, editing, deleting, importing, or restoring syncs automatically in the background. Changes made on one device appear on your others within a few seconds.

The dot by your email shows sync status: green = synced, amber = saving, gray = offline, red = sync problem. The app keeps working offline and catches up when you reconnect.

Sign Out clears this device so the next shooter starts clean — use it on shared computers. Your records stay safe in the cloud and return when you sign back in.
